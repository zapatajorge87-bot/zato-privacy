Privacy Policy - ZATO Copilot Bot

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Introduction

ZATO Copilot Bot operates an automated messaging bot on Instagram. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our bot through Instagram Direct Messages.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with our bot, we may receive the following information provided by the Instagram Messaging API: your Instagram username and profile information, messages you send to our bot, timestamps of interactions, and media shared in the conversation.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to respond to your messages and provide automated assistance, improve the quality of our bot responses, and comply with Instagram/Meta platform policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with Meta/Instagram as required by their platform to facilitate messaging.

5. Data Retention

We retain conversation data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your data by visiting our Data Deletion Page or by sending a message to our bot requesting data deletion.

7. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
