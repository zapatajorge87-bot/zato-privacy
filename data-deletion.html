How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to request the deletion of your data collected through your interactions with ZATO Copilot Bot on Instagram, you can do so using any of the following methods:

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to zapatajorge87@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include your Instagram username.

Option 2: Instagram Direct Message

Send a direct message to our Instagram account requesting data deletion.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will delete all personal data associated with your Instagram account, including: conversation history, profile information stored by our bot, and any media shared during conversations.

Processing Time

Your data deletion request will be processed within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation once your data has been deleted.
